Gran Melia Victoria

Gran Meliá Victoria has an ideal location in central Palma, offering views of its marina, just 100 m away. This hotel features indoor and outdoor swimming pools.

Many of the rooms at this Gran Meliá offer views of Palma Bay. A bathrobe and slippers are also included. Air conditioning or heating is featured in the rooms according to the season.

The Meliá Victoria’s Marivent restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and the Ikatza Restaurant serves dinners. The cocktail bar has a terrace offering views of the marina and the Cathedral.
